We are five Egyptian students from the Faculty of Computer Science and Information, Damanhour University. Driven by our passion for technology and social development, we created this project to raise awareness about sustainable development and promote volunteerism. Through our combined efforts, we aim to highlight critical global issues and inspire positive change in our communities.

Why We Choose This Subject?

We focused on sustainable development and volunteering as the core of our project because these two key concepts are essential for making the world a better place, yet they are often underappreciated, especially in Egypt. Although the public may be highly aware of the significant potential of their involvement, people still fail to value sustainability and volunteer work due to a lack of effective communication.

Our goal is to initiate an awareness campaign that will educate community members and encourage actions leading to a healthier environment, a thriving economy, and social development. Countries that promote sustainable development and volunteerism often experience the most remarkable growth and progress, as engaged citizens have the power to devise creative solutions and take collective responsibility.
