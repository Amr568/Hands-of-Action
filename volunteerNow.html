Want to give your time?

The web is a great tool that helps us get in touch with people in need who are close as well as ones who are far away. At one moment, you are talking to the animal home on the other side of the globe, and before you know it, you are holding a smartphone app that links you with a group of volunteers that need some extra hands in Africa. It doesn't matter if you are a group that needs people, or a volunteer in need of a new project, here are 15 websites that match people with organizations in need.

How to find volunteer opportunities

Before you start the application process, please spend some time reading thoroughly the opportunity descriptions. I know that you are tempted to apply for positions as soon as you come across something new, nonetheless, believe me, the accurate knowledge of exactly what they are seeking from someone is the key as it will save you time in the future. Besides, you can also find the projects that are really in your interest and those that are based on your schedule so you can take part in them. When you’re all set to apply, think of it as a dialogue with them rather than a mere documentation submission. To be clear, they aim to get to know the real you! If the interviewers ask about your motivation for the job or the skills you have, express your natural thoughts and experiences. Keep in mind that those who are looking for passionate people who are involved in their charity programs may be you, too! Maybe just who you are!

15 sites for finding your next volunteer opportunity:

Point

Point has many types of volunteers to choose from, thus, it meets the desires, talents, and these include but are not limited to: youth work, art and music, healthcare, construction, fundraising, and social action. It also provides services ranging from environment protection, education, and homeless help to health care for the volunteers' perfect match.

Do Something

DoSomething centered on mobilizing the youth, shaking off their inhibition and turning them into active agents of change—all thanks to smart technology. The primary concern of environmental problems has been the creation of global warming which could ultimately lead to climate change. Greenhous gases mainly make up for the uplift of temperature, especially with regards to the carbon dioxide emissions that cause large-scale deforestation, the excessive use of coal plants, car exhausts, etc. Besides, it makes the already marginalized community even more unsafe. Another issue is the mental health status of individuals who have to face this and the public's education toward their healthcare.

VolunteerMatch

VolunteerMatch is among the largest volunteer databases for matching nonprofits and volunteers (leaders and followers) together. Since it came into existence fourteen years ago, it has linked 15.2 million participants to organizations around the world, which is really impressive. VolunteerMatch provides two ways, namely, posting volunteer roles or searching for them and has made it super easy to use the site. Besides schools and non-U.S. NGOs, also government agencies, and other taxation-exempt organizations are welcome to apply for a volunteer ad. Also,!

JustServe

JustServe is an online platform that allows people to find volunteer opportunities in their area. One can find projects organizations to search by for the specific volunteer thing of one's interest. Also, the week is a period when organizations and prospective volunteers can explore the published success stories. These are some of them: the ones they presented - a story about a field day, a photo of the volunteers and the date of the event.

Encore.org

Encore promotes the people who are more than half a century to find a new minimum contribution of time, effort, energy, or interest in worthwhile causes. A part of Encore's task is to reshape the notion that the elderly are given a passive role in their neighborhoods. Furthermore, they not only cooperate with the rest of them but also become mentors and motivators for the young volunteers. Encore provides a simple route to locate and participate in local projects, with an interactive map that shows the projects in your vicinity.

Catchafire

Catchafire is the one that helps organizations to volunteer with skills like accounting, branding, engineering, web design, sales, marketing, writing, and social media. If you are a person who has knowledge of accounting, branding, engineering, web design, marketing, writing, social media, etc., then your services are in demand. A major portion of Catchafire's opportunities are online, and so busy professionals have the chance to give their time when it is possible for them.

GozAround

At GozAround ,volunteers take part in activities, during which they can upload their preferences and skills so that their interests, skills, schedules, and location are matched perfectly. With the other components of gamification, which include awarding the volunteers for the number of hours spent and the time of their giving, GozAround makes it an enjoyable experience. The GozAround networking platform is also used to disseminate information on volunteering benefits to the public. Your life may grant you the opportunity to communicate your messages in different forms such as social media and GozAround can help you to systematically increase the organizations' and volunteers' visibility about the important causes.

Volunteer.gov

Volunteer The many legitimate job listings for conservation and animal protection listed will be very interesting for U.S. volunteers, who are inclined towards activities related to nature. The Volunteer.gov website will become a great discovery site for nature lovers alongside a handful of outdoor city activities, including greening and health referral systems. Such website users also have the opportunity to choose the type of assignments they are interested in. Before they start, volunteers can gain experience by reading the guidance articles. Besides, project coordinators will be leaders, who will be working hands-on with volunteers.

Taproot Foundation

Taproot Foundation first of all looks for skillful partners to guide their projects through the process of gaining necessary professional and business knowledge. Organizations are thus recruiting applicants who can aid in social media strategy, employee handbook writing, business plan making, technology examinations, and other assignments. Taproot has a variety of means for discovering the proper volunteering position and thus creating an impact in the lives of others.

Doing Good Together

Besides the standard charity organizations, families can also do their part in the community through Doing Good Together. It is a program that is meant to involve both parents and children in the theme of selfless service, thus Doing Good Together is a family-oriented volunteering organization. It currently includes New York, Oakland, St. Louis, Seattle, Silicon Valley, and the Twin Cities, where one can get free listings for volunteer opportunities. Residents living in these areas can use the location option to find the most suitable place to have family activities.

Create the Good by AARP

Many people are familiar with AARP, the American Association of Retired Person. What some people may not know is this nonprofit offers a database of volunteer opportunities. Named Create the Good, AARP’s volunteer search has more than 200,000 people in a network. It connects charities with volunteers through a simple online search. Registering on Create the Good can help you find new opportunities that match your interests and skills.

United Way

United Way is one of the oldest and most esteemed volunteer organizations. They’ve been influencing positive change for more than 125 years. There are a number of ways to get involved in United Way projects locally. Using the United Way volunteer search tool, you can browse opportunities by keyword and location. Then, you can filter by skill, category and activity type to find a specific opportunity that suits you and your interests.

GivePulse

GivePulse is a search tool that lets both volunteers and volunteer organizations find the help they’re looking for. Organizations, for example, can list a specific call for volunteers and add the location, dates and details of the role. GivePulse helpers can also track their hours on the website, which can then be verified by the organization. Volunteers can collaborate with friends, partners and family members on GivePulse projects in order to make an even larger impact.

Jumpstart

Jumpstart is an education-focused nonprofit that connects college students and community volunteers to students in need. Jumpstart focuses on improving literacy by having educated volunteers read to students, teach reading skills and ignite their interest in books. Volunteers interested in getting involved can join the corps, where they learn the training, skills and practical experience required to be better prepared for the workforce. This is especially relevant to young people interested in education careers, but anyone who loves books and reading is encouraged to apply.

Globe Aware

At Globe Aware , travelers can make an impact in destinations like Thailand, Costa Rica and Peru. Globe Aware offers many specific types of volunteer opportunities, such as those for families, retirees, solo travelers and university groups. Globe Aware is committed to helping people take trips that give back rather than going on another ordinary vacation. By combining beautiful and enriching destinations with meaningful volunteer opportunities, globe aware ensures that everyone involved gets more than they bargained for.

By volunteering, you not only make a difference in the lives of others, but you also gain valuable experience and contribute to the development of a sustainable and educated society.
